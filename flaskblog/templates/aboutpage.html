About the Site

Born to Blog is a blogging site developed using Flask, HTML, CSS, Sqlite.

   

This site was designed to act as a platform that allows multiple contributors from various technical backgrounds to share their ideas and experiences.

Contributor Account Services

  • Login/Sign Up
  • Creating a new account by providing mail address and an account password.
  • Providing a custom Profile Picture for an account.(optional)
  • Visiting Account Page to display account details.
  • Updating User Information.
  • Forgot Password/Changing Account Credentials through link to mailing address.
  • Remember Me option during login.
  • Viewing Top Contributors on the Blog.(by posts and likes)

Blog Posts

  • Creating a new post by providing title and content of post.
  • Displaying all Posts in the Blog in batches of pages.(Latest First)
  • Viewing all posts by a particular contributor.
  • Liking a Post
  • Updation/Deletion of a post, by contributor.
  • Viewing Top Posts(by likes) on the Blog.
  • Filtering Posts posted between two given dates.

The QuickLinks can be used to quickly access certain pages.
